DCHS Hosts Smart-sponsored Training


by Samuel Chueh (January 23, 2017)

Davao Christian High School entertained and hosted 16 different school teachers and administrators from Mindanao during the Central Visayan Institution Foundation-Dynamic Learning Program (CVIF-DLP) Training and Benchmarking Program held in the V. Mapa Campus last January 17-19, 2017. The event, sponsored by Smart Communications Inc., brought educators from all over the country to the school campus to learn the process of planning, making, and evaluating school materials under the DLP System. Junior High School Instructional Leader, Desi Magnaye, lead the participants into experiencing independent learning first hand in classrooms as students and also gave them the chance to plan, write, and adapt the system into their own home schools. Experienced teachers and subject coordinators from the Junior and Senior High School Departments also participated in panel discussions and focus groups to aid the visitors in their training. DLP or Independent Learning (as referred to in DCHS) is an educational method developed by Drs. Christopher and Victoria Bernido which uses parallel learning groups, activity-based multi-domain learning, in-school comprehensive student portfolio, and strategic study & rest periods. DLP is sponsored by Smart Communications Inc. as a part of their social contribution arm.
