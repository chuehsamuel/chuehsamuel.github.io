DCHS Hosts Smart-sponsored Training

January 23, 2017

Davao Christian High School hosted the Smart-sponsored, Dynamic Learning Program - Training & Benchmarking at its V. Mapa campus last January 17-19, 2017. The event immersed participants in independent learning and also trained them to plan, write, and adapt the system into their own home schools. Subject coordinators from High School Department also participated in panel discussions to aid the visitors in their training. DLP or Independent Learning (as referred to in DCHS) is an educational method developed by Drs. Christopher and Victoria Bernido with the aim of improving the quality of education in the Philippines. DLP is sponsored by Smart Communications Inc. as a part of their social contribution arm.

Student Begin the Year Spiritually Anew

January 19, 2017

Students from all levels began 2017 with the traditional 3-day Spiritual Emphasis. This year, the Christian Education Department prepared activities and events in line with the theme, “Power Labs: Discovering Jesus’ Miraculous Power.” Students learned about how Jesus is our ultimate superhero and how we can use our lives to shine for him. Other activities included film showing, crafts, prayer time, and a Bible Quiz Bee. Students made craft robots and also created a Periodic Table of the Bible Books. A memory verse challenge was also issued to encourage students and faculty to memorize 10 verses before the end of the 3 days. Spiritual Emphasis is an annual school custom to start the New Year with a call to return to God in faith -- an event line with the school's mission & objective to foster students with high Christian ideals.
