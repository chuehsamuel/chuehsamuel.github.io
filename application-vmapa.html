New Student Application


THE V. MAPA CAMPUS CURRENTLY OFFERS EDUCATION FOR:

  • Preschool
  • Elementary
  • Junior High School
  • Senior High School

REQUIREMENTS FOR NEW STUDENTS

For Filipino Students:

  1. Birth Certificate (photocopy)
  2. Report Card (original and photocopy)
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  4. Two pieces of 1”x1” colored ID picture

For International Students:

  1. Passport (original and photocopy)
  2. Visa or ACR I-Card (original and photocopy)
  3. School Records in English (notarized)
  4. Two pieces of 1”x1” colored ID picture
  5. Special Study Permit (once accepted)

APPLICATION PROCEDURE

  1. Submit all the requirements to the Registrar prior to the entrance exam day.
  2. Pay the entrance exam fee of Php 250 to the Cashier.
  3. Schedule and take the entrance exam at the Guidance Office.*
  4. Wait for the release of the entrance exam results**.
  5. Undergo an interview with the principal.


*Preschool applicants must take step 5 (interview) before step 3 (entrance exam)
**Exam results are released on the same day except for Grades 1, 2, and 8

Applicants must successfully pass the exam and interview to be accepted. Once accepted, they may reserve slots by paying a non-refundable and non-transferable confirmation fee of Php 2000. This fee, accepted on a first-come-first-serve basis, will be deducted from the tuition fee during enrollment. Reserved slots will be held until the end of the regular enrollment period.
